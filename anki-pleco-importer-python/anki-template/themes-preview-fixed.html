Font Considerations for Chinese Text

Each theme uses carefully selected Chinese fonts optimized for different contexts: Sans-serif fonts (PingFang SC, Noto Sans) provide modern clarity, Serif fonts (STSong, SimSun) offer traditional academic appeal, Brush/Kaiti fonts (STKaiti, BiauKai) evoke calligraphy and cultural heritage. Font choice significantly affects reading comprehension and aesthetic appeal in Chinese language learning.
