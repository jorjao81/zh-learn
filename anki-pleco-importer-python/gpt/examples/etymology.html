Origin: Phono-semantic compound (形声字)

  • Traditional 憶 is analyzed as 从忄，意声: the semantic component 忄 “heart, mind” signals mental activity, and the phonetic component 意 gives the sound yì.
  • Simplified 忆 replaces the right-side 意 with a compact sign 乙 as a standard simplification. The pronunciation and phonetic lineage are clearer in the traditional form (with 意), so treat 意 as the true phonetic for the family.

The Semantic Evolution:

  • “heart/mind + idea” → “to recall/remember.” The base meaning focuses on bringing something back to mind.
  • Developed common nouns/compounds for “memory” or “recollection.” Examples: 记忆 “memory, to remember”; 回忆 “to recollect; recollection.”
  • Path: 忄 “heart/mind” (semantic) + 意 (phonetic) → “to recall/keep in mind” → “memory; recollection.”
